About SquishEyez

Adopt a Squisheye. Born from the “Big Squish,” these beings evolved from single-pixel organisms into curious lifeforms that slipped into early-90s PCs. When high-res graphics outpaced them, they found a new refuge as NFTs on the WAX blockchain.

Series & Art. The genesis set SquishEyez features 69 hand-made 1/1 pieces by @buttzwithaZ, with traits like Filtered, Pixelated, and Gameboy to honor their crunchy pixel roots. Expect 8-bit stills, snappy animation, and delightfully weird audio. SquishEyeZ ² comes in shard packs with the drops and blends available below. Each pack contains a random set of 6 shardz top and bottom for each design. Use Blend Diskettez to create season 2 SquishEyez.

Community & Perks. Holders get competitions, airdrops, and DAO-style participation. Special pieces #1, #21, #25 unlock extra drops via EOS/WAX. #67–#69 are reserved for giveaways. “SquishEyez 01” is a 10/10 promo tied to pre-sale activities (via @ribbit_artwork & @Squisheyez).

Minting & Rights. Minted via NeftyBlocks and AtomicHub. Funded by Next Door Media (UK). Owners can display and distribute the artwork/digital asset; the creator retains authorship and derivative distribution rights.
