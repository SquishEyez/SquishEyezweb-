How SquishEyez Works

Shardz → Blend Diskette → Complete SquishEye

Each complete SquishEye is crafted by combining a Top Shard and a Bottom Shard that share the same trait set. Feed both into the appropriate Blend Diskette to mint the finished piece.

If you’re missing a half, hop into our Discord and post in the shard-swap channel—list your wallet and the shard traits you want to trade. Trades are advertised in Discord, but completed via AtomicHub Offers and Swap. This keeps swaps safe and on-chain.
