About

SquishEyez is a playful NFT collection on the WAX blockchain—slimey avatars with giant eyes and bigger personalities. Born from chunky pixels and DOS-era charm, SquishEyez evolved into a modern collectible universe with seasons, blends, drops, and farms.

Created by @buttzwithaZ, the art leans into 8-bit nostalgia, trading-card vibes, and arcade culture. Season 1 introduced 1/1s; Season 2 expands via Shardz + Diskettez that you combine in WaxDAO blends to craft styles like Pixelz and Doodlez.
