SquishEyez NFT Collection

About

Welcome to the SquishEyez Universe! A playful NFT collection on the WAX blockchain featuring charming, slime-like avatars infused with vibrant personality. What began as single-pixel creatures has evolved into an expressive and collectible art series.

Created by artist @buttzwithaZ, the collection blends DOS-era graphics, trading-card aesthetics, and 8-bit nostalgia with modern digital art. Season one introduced 69 unique 1/1 SquishEyez; new seasons expand the universe through interactive blends, exclusive drops, and community events.
