About SquishEyez

Welcome to the SquishEyez universe! A playful NFT collection on the WAX blockchain, SquishEyez features charming, slime-like avatars with big personality. What began as single-pixel creatures has evolved into an expressive, collectible art series. Take custody of a SquishEye today.

Origins. In a distant corner of the Squishiverse, a species called SquishEyez emerged from a cosmic event known as the Big Squish. Starting as single-pixel organisms, they adapted to a realm where physics was as flexible as their forms—until their universe began to collapse into larger cosmic forces.

The Great Migration. As the Squishiverse contracted, the last 69 originals—now known as Squisheyez—sought refuge on Earth. In the early 1990s they slipped into the burgeoning digital world, nesting inside DOS systems and “borrowing” the eyes of video-game characters to explore and map their new home.

Survival in the Digital Age. The leap from 2D pixels to 3D polygons—“Polygonz”—threatened their existence. Resilient as ever, they found permanence on the WAX blockchain, minting themselves as ssquisheyezz to outlive hardware and format shifts.

The SSQuisheyezz Mission. Today, each SSQuisheyezz seeks a new host to help it evolve. Collectors join a community dedicated to preserving the species and advancing their story through a DAO-driven ecosystem.

Created by artist @buttzwithaZ, SquishEyez blends DOS-era graphics, trading-card vibes, and 8-bit nostalgia with modern digital art. Season one introduced 69 unique 1/1s; new seasons expand the universe with interactive blends, curated drops, and community events.
